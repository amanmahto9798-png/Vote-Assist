📅 Historical Indian Elections

1952
First General Election. INC wins 364/489 seats. Jawaharlal Nehru becomes PM.
1977
First non-Congress government. Janata Party wins 295 seats after Emergency era.
1991
Held amid Rajiv Gandhi assassination. INC wins 244 seats. P.V. Narasimha Rao becomes PM.
2014
BJP wins historic 282-seat majority. Narendra Modi becomes PM. Turnout: 66.4%.
2019
BJP re-elected with 303 seats. Record turnout of 67.4%. Modi returns as PM.
2024
NDA coalition wins 293 seats. BJP gets 240. Modi serves third term as PM.

Typical Indian Election Timeline

  • Announcement: Election Commission declares schedule
  • Nominations: Candidates file their papers
  • Campaigning: Rallies and public meetings
  • Polling: Held in multiple phases across the country
  • Counting Day: Votes tallied and results announced
  • Government Formation: Winning coalition forms government

Voter Guide

Your Voting Guidelines
✅

Pros of Voting

  • Shape your community's future
  • Hold elected officials accountable
  • Impact policies on health, education & economy
  • Exercise your fundamental democratic right
⚠️

Cons / Challenges

  • Time-consuming process (lines, research)
  • Information overload and misinformation
  • Feeling like one vote doesn't matter (it does!)
🚫

What NOT to Do as a Voter

  • ❌ Don't rely on a single source of news. Verify from multiple credible sources.
  • ❌ Don't skip local elections. Local officials have the most direct impact on daily life.
  • ❌ Don't share unverified information. Stop the spread of fake news on social media.
  • ❌ Don't wait until the last minute. Register early and know your ID requirements.
  • ❌ Don't let apathy win. Your vote adds to the mandate and shapes party platforms.
